Use these with your preferred image gen (Midjourney, DALL-E, Flux,
Stable Diffusion, Sora, Imagen). All renders should be PNG with
transparent background. Output dimensions ~1600×900 (16:9-ish, but
wider helps on landscape monitors).

Save outputs into your HA setup at:
  /config/www/tesla/car/<filename>.png

Then they'll be served at /local/tesla/car/<filename>.png

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  GENERATION TIPS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• Generate VARIANT 1 first. Once you nail the angle/color/composition,
  use that exact image as a reference (img2img / character reference)
  for the other variants. This keeps the car identical across states.

• Stealth Grey is suggested because it photographs well in all
  lighting. If your actual car is a different color, swap "Stealth
  Grey" → your color (Pearl White / Diamond Black / Glacier Blue /
  Ultra Red / Quicksilver).

• If your image gen struggles with "Juniper" specifics, attach a
  reference image of the 2026 Model Y front fascia.

• The car layer is the hero of the dashboard. Spend the most prompt
  iteration here. The other layers (sky/weather) are shared with
  the original sky_system and don't need new assets.
